Creamy Lemon Parmesan Chicken


Description

This lemon Parmesan chicken recipe is an “easy, yet impressive, dish to create,” according to recipe tester Nathan.
This dish can be done in 40 mintues and provides 4 servings with 874 calories and 68 grams of protein per serving ! A nice chicken pasta
with some cheese and lemon. A meal that can be made so quick even a child could do this with a blindfold.

(Please don't actually have a child alone in the kitchen without supervision. Especially with a blindfold.)


Ingredients

  • 3/4 grated parmesan cheese and 1/2 cup mascarpone

  • 1/4 cup flour

  • 8 skinless boneless chicken thighs

  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt and black pepper

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 and 3/4 cups of low sodium chicken broth

  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest, 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice, and 4 lemon wedges for serving.

  • 1 tablespoon Cornstarch

  • Chopped oregano to taste

Directions

  1. Mix 1/4 cup of Parmesan with flour and season the chicken.

  2. Cover the chicken in the flour mixture.

  3. Brown the chicken on all sides and remove from skillet. After all the chicken is browned use the oil still in pan to cook the garlic.

  4. Mix the broth, mascarpone, lemon zest and juice, and remaining parmesan.

  5. Add the mixture to the skillet and bring to a boil.

  6. Make a cornstarch slurry and whisk into the broth mixture

  7. Add the chicken back into the mixutre and cook until no longer pink. Depending on the thickness of the chicken thigh take extra precaution
    in making sure it is throughly cooked. You could also cut the chikcen into pieces before putting into mixture to make it cook faster.

  8. Plate and garnish with the herbs of your choosing and place a lemon wedge on the side.
